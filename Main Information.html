Key Information

Health

Public health services in Brazil are free for foreign tourists. So, if you are in an accident or present any health problems, just call the Mobile Emergency Service dialing 192 on the phone.

Security

Most tourists have a good experience when arriving in Brazil, but, especially in the big cities, you must be careful not to become targeted by thugs.

Visa and Documents Required

Citizens from Mercosur member counties are not required to present visa, only the passport or a valid identification document.

Emergency Numbers

Cardiovascular diseases can be triggered anytime, and the difference between saving a life and losing it matters on how long it takes to get the help, so remember numbers to contact in case of need

Electricity

Many hotels offer wall sockets in both voltages, and it is easy to find portable voltage transformers in construction shops

Telecommunications

The National Telecommunications Agency is the Government agency in charge of administering phone operations where you can learn more about codes, carriers and other information.
